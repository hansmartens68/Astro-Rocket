Building a great website used to mean choosing between starting from scratch or wrestling with a bloated template that does too much and lets you control too little.

Astro Rocket is built around a different idea: give you everything you actually need, nothing you don't, and make every piece easy to understand and change.

## What is Astro Rocket?

Astro Rocket is a professional website theme built on [Astro](https://astro.build) — the modern framework built for content-driven websites. It's not a minimal starter that leaves the hard work to you, and it's not an everything-and-the-kitchen-sink template that takes weeks to learn. It sits exactly in between: complete out of the box, clean enough to make your own.

It ships with:

- A **full blog** powered by MDX and Astro's Content Collections
- A **component library** covering every section a professional website needs
- A **complete SEO layer** with structured data, Open Graph tags, and sitemap
- **Dark mode** that works without a flash, without JavaScript dependencies
- A **contact form** with Resend email delivery
- **One-click Vercel deployment** with all environment variables documented

## Built on the right foundation

Astro Rocket runs on [Astro 6](https://astro.build), [Tailwind CSS v4](https://tailwindcss.com), and TypeScript. These aren't trendy choices — they're the most capable tools available for building fast, maintainable websites right now.

Astro ships minimal JavaScript to the browser by default. Pages are rendered to HTML at build time, which means visitors get your content instantly — no loading spinners, no hydration delay, no blank screens. On a good connection, pages feel immediate. On a slow one, they're still fast.

Tailwind v4 brings a CSS-first configuration model that makes it simpler to maintain a consistent design system across a large project. And TypeScript keeps your configuration and components predictable — if something doesn't match the expected shape, you know at build time, not in production.

## Who is it for?

Astro Rocket works for a wide range of projects.

**Freelancers and designers** building their portfolio. You get a professional starting point with a blog, an about section, and a contact form — all styled and ready to customise.

**Developers** who want a solid, well-structured codebase they can understand and extend. The code is clean, the patterns are consistent, and nothing is hiding behind magic.

**Small businesses** who need a fast, dependable website with a contact form, clear navigation, and content they can update themselves.

**People learning Astro** who want to see a real, complete project — not just a tutorial with five pages.

## Everything in place from day one

The thing that takes the most time when starting a new website isn't the code — it's all the small decisions. How do you structure the navigation? How do you handle the blog? What does the SEO layer look like? How does the contact form connect to email?

Astro Rocket has already made those decisions and implemented them well. You spend your time on your content and your design — not on rebuilding infrastructure that should come standard.
